THE GREEN CLUB

Somaiya Vidyavihar starts a Green Club- It is an initiative taken taken up by the Department of Environmental sciences at KJ somaiya college of science and commerce. The club under the guidance of the department of environmental science is a student based group committed to sustainability initiatives on campus. The Club was inaugurated by Nobel Peace prize winner Dr John Byrne, Director of the Center for Energy and Environmental Policy (CEEP) and Distinguished Professor of Energy and Climate Policy at the University of Delaware, who spoke to the students on 'Climate Change and Need for Sustainable Energy'.
